SDS 387: Theory of Linear Models

Alessandro (Ale) Rinaldo - Fall, 2024

SDS 387 is an intermediate graduate course in theoretical statistics for PhD students, covering two separate but interrelated topics: (i) stochastic convergence and (ii) linear regression modeling. The material and style of the course will skew towards the mathe- matical and theoretical foundations of common models and methods, designed to provide a foundation for those who wish to pursue research in statistical methods and theory. This is not an applied regression analysis course.
