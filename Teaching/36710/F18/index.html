Course Description and Objectives:
This is a core Ph.D. course in theoretical statistics. We will cover a selection of modern topics in mathematical statistics, with a focus on high-dimensional statistical models and non-parametric statistical models. One of the main goals of this course is to provide you with some theoretical background and mathematical tools to read and understand the current statistical literature on high-dimensional models.
